# Sources for table group "4" (main_1.csv)

These are the literature papers the 29 new samples (`4_1`–`4_29`) in `main_1.csv` were
extracted from. Composition and hydrogen-capacity data were pulled from each paper's tables
(occasionally recovered from Excel date-corruption or read off a figure — see
`merge_experimental_data.ipynb` for exact provenance notes per row).

| Table IDs | Paper | PDF |
|---|---|---|
| `4_1`–`4_5` | Effect on the activation and hydrogen storage properties of Y–TiFe-based composites with vanadium via mechanical milling | `papers/1-s2.0-S0360319925004914-main.pdf` |
| `4_6`–`4_11` | Microstructural feature and hydrogen storage properties of TiFe0.7Mn0.2X0.1 (X = V, Cr, Co, Ni, Cu) hydrogen storage alloy | `papers/1-s2.0-S0360319925010699-main.pdf` |
| `4_12`–`4_23` | Optimizing hydrogen storage in TiFe–V alloys: Influence of sample purity and heat treatment | `papers/1-s2.0-S0360319925025170-main.pdf` |
| `4_24` | Hydrogen storage and stability of rare earth-doped TiFe alloys under extensive cycling | `papers/1-s2.0-S036031992502292X-main.pdf` |
| `4_25`–`4_28` | Enhanced initial hydrogenation of TiFe-based hydrogen storage alloys containing C | `papers/1-s2.0-S0925838825052776-main.pdf` |
| `4_29` | Hydrogen Storage in TiFe-Based Alloys Doped with Cr and S: Thermodynamic and Structural Peculiarities (Korol et al., SSRN preprint 5346127) | `papers/ssrn-5346127.pdf` |

Note: the Cr/S paper (`4_29`) originally reported 2 alloys; the S-containing one
(TiFe0.9Cr0.08S0.02) was excluded because `main.csv` has no sulfur column.
